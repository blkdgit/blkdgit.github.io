Welcome!

By Balakhilya das, posted February 1st 2017

To all of you out there who have found my web site; welcome.
I am very glad that you are here. It is my hope that you will be able to take something with you that will act as a guiding light in your life.

Myself, I came to this philosophy of truth in an indirect way. I started becoming interested in hatha yoga (physical yoga) as a way to improve my surfing which at that time (1969) was my entire life. The benefits were immediately obvious and I started to look deeper into the subject. By reading I began to practice simple meditation, I adopted a vegetarian yoga diet, and spent more and more time in deeper contemplation of the truths of life. I had always been a traveler, but now I embarked on an inner journey. All the while my world, both my external and internal, became increasingly peaceful and calm.

In 1971 I had the incredibly good fortune (by Divine arrangement I am sure) to come in contact with my Spiritual Masters. Under their expert guidence, I received and started following the ancient wisdom of Bhakti Yoga which I have continued to do until this day. And what a beautiful and fullfilling journey it has been!

So I sincerely wish that you may have the same fortune and blessing that I have had, undeserving as I am.
All the best.

Balakhilya das
